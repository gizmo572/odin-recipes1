Watermelon Recipe

Description

Watermelon is a fruit, but it is also a color! You can grow it, but this will take months of laborous work. This recipe will show you how to make your own watermelon in just minutes!

Ingredients

  • Red
  • Blue
  • Yellow

Steps

  1. Add equal parts Red and Yellow to get ORANGE
  2. Use sorcery to create WHITE
  3. Mix equal parts red, white, and orange to get WATERMELON!!!
